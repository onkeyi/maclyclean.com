Is Smart Scan really free forever?
Yes — no catch. Smart Scan (Junk Files module) is completely free with no time limit, no account required, and no credit card. We believe every Mac user should be able to clean basic junk for free.
What does the 7-day trial include?
The trial unlocks all 11 modules for 7 days at no cost. After the trial, Smart Scan stays free and you can purchase a license at any time to restore full access.
Is it safe? Will it delete important files?
Macly always shows you exactly what it found before deleting anything. Most modules move files to Trash so you can restore them. Junk Files (caches and logs) are permanently deleted since macOS auto-regenerates them — they can't accidentally remove personal documents.
Which macOS versions are supported?
Macly requires macOS 13 Ventura or later. It supports both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs.
How does the license work?
A license key is delivered to your email after purchase. Enter it once in Macly → it's stored securely in your Keychain. One license covers 1, 3, or up to 10 Macs depending on the tier you choose. No subscription, no renewal.
Why does Mail scanning need Full Disk Access?
Apple Mail stores attachments inside ~/Library/Mail, which is protected by macOS's TCC system. Full Disk Access lets Macly read that folder. You grant it once in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Full Disk Access — Macly never sends your data anywhere.
